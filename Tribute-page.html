Microsoft History


  • 1975 - Microsoft was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

  • 1980 - Microsoft began a partnership with IBM to bundle its operating system with IBM's computers.

  • 1986 - Bill Gates and Paul Allen bought a chicken farm in Redmond, Washington to start building there headquarter campus. The Campus spans 500 acres.

  • 2001 - Microsoft announces the xbox which later goes on to be the sole console competitor to playstation. There are now millions of xbox consoloes in homes around the world.

  • 2019 - Microsoft is the second company in the world to be worth one trillion dollars.

  • 2020 - Microsoft invests one billion dollars in Open-ai which has turned Microsoft into the leader in AI based applications from web search to all of its application.

Microsoft's current ceo, Satya Nadella, shares that everyone should have empathy.
